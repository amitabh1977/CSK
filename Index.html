Our Menu

Chai

Masala Chai

Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of our Masala Chai, a traditional Indian tea infused with a blend of spices. Crafted with care and expertise, our Masala Chai offers a delightful sensory experience that will transport you to the streets of India with every sip.

Ginger Chai

Our Adrak Chai is characterized by its robust tea base and the unmistakable kick of ginger. The earthy notes of Assam tea blend seamlessly with the heat of freshly grated ginger, creating a harmonious fusion of flavors that awaken the senses and leave a lingering warmth.

Elachi Chai

Our Elaichi Chai is characterized by its smooth and creamy texture, balanced by the flavor of green cardamom. The robustness of Assam tea melds with the citrusy and floral notes of cardamom, resulting in a blend that tantalizes the taste buds leaving a lasting impression.

Samosas

Punjabi Samosa

The Punjabi Samosa boasts a robust and savory flavor profile, with the perfect balance of spices and vegetables. The tender potatoes, sweet peas, and spices are encased in a golden, flaky pastry shell, creating a delightful textures and flavors that's impossible to resist.

Jain Samosa

Enjoy our Jain Samosa as a delicious appetizer or snack, accompanied by our refreshing beverages. Whether you follow a Jain diet or simply appreciate the flavors of authentic Indian cuisine, our Jain Samosa promises to satisfy your cravings and leave you craving for more.

Party Samosa

Our Party Samosas are a crowd-pleasing favorite, perfect for gatherings, celebrations, and special occasions. These larger-sized samosas are handcrafted with care and packed with flavor, making them the ideal choice for sharing with friends and family.

Savouries

Vada Pav

To prepare our Vada Pav, we start by making the potato vada mixture, which is seasoned with a blend of spices and then shaped into patties. These patties are then dipped in a chickpea flour batter and deep-fried until golden and crispy. The pav bread is lightly toasted and then filled with the potato vada, with a generous drizzle of chutney.

Dabeli

Our Dabeli is a symphony of sweet, spicy, and tangy flavors, with the richness of the potato filling complemented by the crunch of peanuts, the sweetness of tamarind chutney, and the heat of garlic chutney. Each bite is a burst of deliciousness, transporting you to the bustling streets of Gujarat with its vibrant and aromatic taste.

Kachori

Our Kachori is a harmonious blend of textures and flavors, with the crispiness of the pastry shell giving way to the savory filling within. Each bite is a burst of aromatic spices and earthy lentils, balanced by the sweetness of peas and the heat of green chilies, creating a taste sensation that's sure to leave you craving for more.
